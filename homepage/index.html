Who are we?

We are a group of individuals that live in foriegn countries that are often at very high temperatures. We crave cold drinks to cool down and balance the hot weather. So we decided as a group to start Slooshys and develop our very own smart machine that'll help with the hot weather.

What are we offering?

We are offering a machine that is capable of making any cold drink from the comfort of your home. With that we offer a plethora of flavours and options to support people’s preferences and physical-health!
